About Me

Welcome to my page. I'm Victor Akindele, a Nigerian who lives in the City of Wolverhampton.
I am a devoted Christian, a graphic designer and a front-end developer in training. My interest in tech has been growing since my university days, that is about 10 years ago. This is because I get fascinated by how you can basically solve any problems by writing codes! I have been an article writer since I was in college. I enjoy writing and designing. I do my graphic designs when writing and I also help people with graphic designs of various form. I knew I could do more and progress in this aspect so enrolled for a font-end development course to unlock designing by writing codes. I value hard work, consistency and solving problems. I love playing video games in my leisure and generally likes fun
